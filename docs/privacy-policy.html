Privacy Policy for BettrMind

Last updated: June 4, 2026

BettrMind ("the app", "we", "us") is a self-help tool that shows you a mindful reminder before you open apps you have chosen to watch — such as gambling or money-spending apps — so you can pause and decide with intention. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using BettrMind, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we do NOT collect

BettrMind is built to keep your information on your own device. We do not collect, sell, or share:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or contacts
  • Your location
  • The content of your screen, messages, or any app you use
  • Photos, files, or media beyond an image you personally choose (see below)
  • Payment, banking, or financial account information

2. Information the app uses on your device

To do its job, BettrMind uses the following — all stored locally on your device:

  • Your list of watched apps: the apps you select to receive a reminder for.
  • Reminder activity: a local log of moments you opened a watched app and whether you chose to pause ("resisted") or continue ("proceeded"), so the app can show your progress to you.
  • Your preferences: such as apps you have muted and an optional motivating image you choose.

This information stays on your device. It is not uploaded to us and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

3. Permissions we request and why

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS): used only to detect which app is currently in the foreground, so a reminder can appear at the right time. It does not read the content of your screen, text, keystrokes, or app data.
  • Notifications & Foreground Service: the reminder is shown as a notification when you open a watched app; the foreground service keeps the detection running reliably and tells you it is active. BettrMind never covers your screen and never blocks any app.
  • Photos (optional): only if you choose to pick a motivating image for your reminder. The image stays on your device.

4. Network access

BettrMind works fully offline. The app does not connect to any server and does not send your watched-app list, reminder activity, or any personal information anywhere. All processing happens entirely on your device.

5. Children's privacy

BettrMind is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Data security

Because your information stays on your device, its security depends largely on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secured and up to date.

7. Not a substitute for professional help

BettrMind is a self-help and responsible-use tool. It does not block payments and is not a medical or counseling service. If gambling or spending is affecting your life, please seek help from a qualified professional.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
