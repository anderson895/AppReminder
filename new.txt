What's New in BettrMind 1.0.0
==============================

• Reminders now work with banking apps. The "before you continue" card
  is shown as its own full screen instead of an overlay, so apps like
  GCash no longer block it. Your reminder appears reliably every time.

• Cleaner reminder card. Removed the "Don't show again today" option so
  the reminder always shows up when you open a watched app.

• Accurate "Days Bet-Free" counter. The streak now counts only from the
  day you started using BettrMind, instead of showing a misleadingly
  large number for new accounts. It grows one day at a time you stay
  bet-free and resets if you open a gambling app.

• Still fully offline. No account sync, no network — your data stays on
  your device.
